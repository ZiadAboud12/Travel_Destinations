Overview

Dubai, a city in the UAE, is famous for its rapid development, luxury lifestyle, and iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa and Palm Jumeirah. Known for its blend of modernity and tradition, Dubai offers world-class shopping, diverse dining, and unique experiences, from desert safaris to luxury resorts. With a strategic location and business-friendly environment, it has become a global hub for tourism, trade, and innovation.
